MARS+

The Mars MD hospital is open to all patients every day and provides fundamental medical care and cutting-edge medicine in a central location in Mumbai. We use our superior academic knowledge to treat a wide range of health issues, taking a personal touch and utilizing highly specialized and up-to-date research. Each year, we treat around 42,000 inpatients and have over 500,000 outpatient visit our institute. Our employees guarantee comprehensive and individualized medical support even beyond their specific disciplines. With 980 hospital beds and close to 300,000 inpatient days, Mars MD is one of the largest hospitals in India.
